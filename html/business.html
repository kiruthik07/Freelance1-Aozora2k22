BUSINESS PLAN

Format

PowerPoint 5 minutes elevator pitch in person

Business plan is: a presentation for why an investor should invest in your company followed by a Q&A. Your pitch can talk about (not exhaustive) your idea, vision, team, market potential and why you are best suited to solve this problem.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

  • Participants may work individually or in groups (maximum Allowed team size: 3 students).
  • There is no limit on the number of teams that can participate from a single institute; however, no student can be a part of more than one team.
  • All decisions regarding eligibility and final judgment shall rest with the judging panel.
  • Each group must prepare a Presentation.
  • Product visual and/or prototype marketing plan.
  • All group members must contribute, participate, and speak during the presentation.
  • The Business plan team reserves the right to change the format or rules of the round before it starts.
